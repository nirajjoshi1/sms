# Complete demo seed manifest

The connected Neon database was reset and seeded with a connected, single-school dataset for full application testing.

## Seeded coverage

- One Nepal-based secondary school and academic session
- Three classes and three matching sections
- Five subjects and subject groups for every class
- Three teachers, each assigned as a class teacher
- Cross-class subject teaching through the weekly timetable
- Accountant and receptionist staff profiles
- Ten students distributed across all classes and sections
- Seventy-five Monday–Friday timetable periods
- Student and staff attendance
- Homework, submissions, exam marks and teacher ratings
- Leave types, a leave request and payroll
- Fee group, fee type, fee master, discount, reminder, payments and an offline bank request
- Income and expense heads with transactions
- General, notification, SMS, email, payment, print and system settings
- Event, gallery, news, media, CMS page, menus and banner
- Certificate and ID-card templates
- Notification, school request, backup metadata and audit log
